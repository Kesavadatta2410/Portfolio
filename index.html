About Me

I'm a passionate 3D designer and tech enthusiast pursuing a degree in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at Woxsen University. As the President of the Ankur Club (Incubation Club), I lead initiatives to foster innovation and support aspiring entrepreneurs.

My expertise lies in 3D modelling and animation, with a focus on Blender, where I bring ideas to life through digital design. I am also strongly interested in IoT, blending design and technology to create impactful solutions.

I'm always eager to collaborate on projects that push the boundaries of AI, design, and IoT, helping bridge the gap between creative concepts and real-world applications. Let's connect and explore opportunities to innovate together!
